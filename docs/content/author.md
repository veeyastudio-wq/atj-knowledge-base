# Vilam Vuong — author biography

I spent a year and a half in the UK family court system fighting to spend more time with my daughter. I didn't understand the process. I didn't know where to begin, who to speak to, or what any of the forms meant. I tried to fill them in myself and couldn't. I went to court without knowing how to prepare or how to represent myself. I used the internet as much as I could, but even that had limits.

Years later, a close friend went through the same thing. He managed to find lawyers, but they didn't serve him well, and he is still struggling with the consequences.

I had been building products using AI. I thought: I went through this. He went through this. Thousands of people are going through this right now. The legal system is not going away, and lawyers are necessary but not for every single step, and not at the cost of an email, a letter, and a phone call billed separately each time.

That is why I built Access to Justice.
