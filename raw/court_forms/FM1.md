# Confirm if you have attended a Mediation Information and Assessment Meeting: Form FM1

Use this form to confirm to the court if you have attended a Mediation Information and Assessment Meeting. If you have not, you must give the reason why.

able to signpost you to other help and support services.
You must have attended a MIAM before making this application unless the requirement to attend a MIAM does not
apply because the Children Act 1989 order you are applying for:

supervision proceedings (or is already the subject of an emergency, care or supervision order); or
• you are exempt from the requirement to attend a MIAM. Where evidence is required to support a claimed
exemption, this evidence must be provided with this application.

evidence is required to support a MIAM exemption claim, this evidence must be provided with your application. For
examples of what evidence is acceptable, please refer to section 2, pages 3-5. When processing your application,
the court will review the evidence provided to ensure that a MIAM exemption has been validly claimed.
