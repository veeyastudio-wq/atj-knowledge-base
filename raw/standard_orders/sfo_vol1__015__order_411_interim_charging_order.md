Recitals

This order was made without a hearing in accordance with Family Procedure Rules, rule 40.5(1).

The judge read the application notice in Form FE6 verified by a statement of truth as required by Family Procedure Rules, Practice Direction 40A.

On [date and time], [name of judge] considered the application of the applicant, from which it appears that:

on [date], [name of judge] made an order under case number [case number] requiring the respondent to pay money to the applicant;

the amount now owing under that order is £[amount] including interest and costs as follows:

the respondent is the owner of, or has a beneficial interest in, the asset described in the schedule below.

IT IS ORDERED THAT:

The interest of the respondent in the asset described in the schedule below shall stand charge with payment of £[amount], together with any further interest becoming due, and the costs of the application.

The application shall be listed for a further hearing before a [District] / [Circuit] / [High Court] Judge at the Family Court sitting at [court name] on [date] at [time] when the judge will decide whether the charge created by this order should continue (with or without modification) or should be discharged.

The applicant shall serve all relevant persons with the interim charging order, the application notice, and all documents filed in support of it in accordance with the Family Procedure Rules, rule 40.6 by 4:00pm on the date 21 days before the hearing listed in paragraph [para number] above.

If the respondent and/or any other person objects to the court making a final charging order, that person shall file with the court and serve on the parties written evidence stating the grounds of objection by 4:00pm on the date 7 days before the hearing listed in paragraph [para number] above.
