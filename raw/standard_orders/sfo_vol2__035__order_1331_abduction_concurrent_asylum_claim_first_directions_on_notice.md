ANNEX 1

THE COURT RESPECTFULLY REQUESTS THAT THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR THE HOME DEPARTMENT:

Allocate the protection claims referred to in this Order to the Home Office Expedited Team.

Indicate to the court and to the parties in writing, preferably by [date and time]:

what further preparatory steps, if any, are required prior to the determination of the protection claim;

whether any protection claim has been made (or can be understood to have been made) in respect of the child; and

where the child is said to have “refugee status”, whether this is a reflection of a determination that the child is a refugee as defined in Art 1A of the 1951 Geneva Convention or as a result of the child being a named dependant of a successful application for protection by a parent.

Keep the court informed with respect to the progress of the protection claim(s) and/or appeals(s) and of any reconsideration of refugee status and, in particular, inform the court of any delays, or requests for extensions of time, in the protection claim.

Make any request to the court to exercise its case management powers that the Secretary of State considers will expedite the protection claim.

Provide the court, preferably by [date and time] with an anticipated timetable for the determination of the protection claim by the Secretary of State.

Ensure that a clear line of communication is maintained between the Secretary of State and the court.

Where the Secretary of State accepts the invitation of the court, as set out in this Order above, to intervene in the child abduction proceedings, attend all hearings by a representative.
