Definitions

The Tipstaff is the enforcement officer of the High Court at the Royal Courts of Justice. They have a deputy and assistants and can authorise police officers to act on their behalf. Any obligation to give information to the Tipstaff or to hand over a document to them includes an obligation to do so to their deputy or assistant or a police officer acting on their behalf.

A Passport Order is an order directed to the Tipstaff authorising them to seize any passport or other travel documents from them.

Note to Arresting Officer:

Under Schedule 2, Part 1, section 4(1) of the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003, breach of a FGM protection order is a criminal offence punishable by up to five years’ imprisonment. It is an arrestable offence and it is not necessary to obtain a warrant.

“A person who without reasonable excuse does anything that the person is prohibited from doing by an FGM protection order is guilty of an offence.”
