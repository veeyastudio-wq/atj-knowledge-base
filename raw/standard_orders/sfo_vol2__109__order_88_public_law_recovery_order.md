The court authorises [name of authorised person] / [a police constable] to remove the child.

Warning

It is an offence intentionally to obstruct the person from removing the child (Section 50(9) Children Act 1989).

The court authorises a police constable to enter the premises known as [full address] and search for the child, using reasonable force if necessary.
