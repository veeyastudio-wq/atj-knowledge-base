IT IS ORDERED THAT:

The respondent shall be committed to prison for [number (not exceeding 42)] days unless [he] / [she] pays the sum stated in paragraph 5 above, or files an affidavit stating that a receiving order or an order of adjudication in bankruptcy has been made against [him] / [her].
