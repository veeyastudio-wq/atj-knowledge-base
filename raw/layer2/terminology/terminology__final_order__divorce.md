# Final order

## What it means

The final order is the court order that legally ends a marriage. Once it is made, both parties are free to remarry. It cannot be applied for until at least six weeks after the conditional order. It is strongly advisable to have any financial arrangements finalised and recorded in a consent order before applying for the final order — once the final order is made, certain financial claims become more difficult to pursue.

## When you will encounter it

At the end of the divorce track, once the six-week waiting period after the conditional order has passed. You apply for it using the online divorce service. The court processes it and sends both parties a certificate confirming the marriage has ended.
