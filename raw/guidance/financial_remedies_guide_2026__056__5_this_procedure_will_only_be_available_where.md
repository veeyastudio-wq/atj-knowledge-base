# 5. This procedure will only be available where:-

(i) an application has been made on the online portal by Form D11 and there is a draft agreed
directions order in the standard form set out in the annex below which is agreed by both
parties and signed by them (or on their behalf);
(ii) the required documents together with the signed draft agreed directions order have been
uploaded to the online portal at least 7 days prior to the date fixed for the First
Appointment hearing; and
(iii) a judge has approved the draft agreed directions order in advance of the hearing.
