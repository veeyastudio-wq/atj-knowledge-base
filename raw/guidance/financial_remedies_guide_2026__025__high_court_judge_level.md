# High Court Judge level

64. Subject to available judicial resources, the FDR Appointment will be listed with a
time estimate of 1 day unless (i) the parties certify, giving written reasons, that a
lesser period is sufficient; and (ii) obtain the written permission of the FDR judge
before whom the case is listed for hearing for the reduced time estimate. The
parties will be directed to attend one hour before the scheduled start time to
commence negotiations.
