# Court

12. The principal aim and objective of the FRC is to improve the delivery of justice
for parties involved in court proceedings relating to financial issues arising from
the ending of their relationship.
13. In order to enhance efficiency in the disposal of financial remedy cases, to ensure
that such cases are allotted an appropriate share of the court’s resources, and
to improve access to justice for all litigants, the standards and procedures as set
out in this Guide must be observed.
