# Circuit Judge and District Judge level

62. Subject to available judicial resources, the FDR Appointment will be listed with a
time estimate of 1 - 1½ hours unless the court at the First Appointment directs a
longer or shorter period. It shall be the responsibility of the zonal Lead Judges to
decide, in consultation with HMCTS, and publicise the time-estimate policy to
operate within their zone.
63. FDR Appointments shall normally be listed in the course of the morning,
ordinarily at 10:00 or 10:30, but parties and their advisers must ensure that they
are available for the whole day. The parties will ordinarily be directed to attend
one hour before the scheduled start time to commence negotiations.
