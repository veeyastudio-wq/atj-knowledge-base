# 11. Urgent hearings

For urgent hearings it may not be possible to prepare a full bundle. Urgent hearings
are when the court receives an application and the judge decides a hearing is
needed as soon as possible - for example that day or sometime in the same week.
In these cases, a party should make sure the judge and the other party have copies
of the key documents they intend to refer to as soon as possible.
