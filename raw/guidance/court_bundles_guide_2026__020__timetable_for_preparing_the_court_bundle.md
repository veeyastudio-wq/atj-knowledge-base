# Timetable for preparing the court bundle

9.4 Documents or bundles should be sent to the court several working days before
the hearing unless the judge orders otherwise. The term “working days” means the
days when people usually go to work. These are typically Monday to Friday.
Weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and public holidays are not considered working
days.
