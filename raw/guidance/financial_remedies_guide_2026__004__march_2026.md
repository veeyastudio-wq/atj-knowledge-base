# March 2026

1. We are authorised by the President to release this Statement.
2. This Guide applies to all cases heard in the Financial Remedies Court (“FRC”)
whether allocated to the level of a High Court Judge (sitting at the Royal Courts
of Justice or elsewhere), or to the level of a Circuit Judge or a District Judge.
