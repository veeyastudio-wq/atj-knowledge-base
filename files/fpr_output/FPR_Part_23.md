### Power to call witness for cross-examination on hearsay evidence E+W


23.4. —(1) Where a party—

### Evidence of consent of trustee to act E+W


23.8. In proceedings to which Part 9 (financial remedies) applies , a document purporting to contain the written consent of a person to act as trustee and to bear that person's signature verified by some other person is evidence of such consent.
